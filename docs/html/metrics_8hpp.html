Compute Sum Squared Total variance of target. In fact, \( n Var(y) \).

\( \displaystyle SST(y) = \sum_{i=1}^{n} (y_{i} - \bar{y})^2 \), where \( y \) is the target vector, \( \bar{y} \) is target vector's mean, \( n \) is the number of predictions.

Coefficient of determination of predictions.

\( \displaystyle R^2 = 1 - \frac{SSE(y, \hat{y})}{SST(y)} \). Can also be expressed as \( \displaystyle R^2 = 1 - \frac{n MSE(y, \hat{y})}{n Var(y)} = 1 - \frac{MSE(y, \hat{y})}{Var(y)} \), where \( SSE \) is Metrics::sse, \( SST \) is Metrics::sst, \( MSE \) is Metrics::mse.
